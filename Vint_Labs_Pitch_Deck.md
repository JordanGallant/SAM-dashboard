## Why Existing Tools Fall Short

### Asset Registry & EAM
- SAP EAM, Trimble, IBM Maximo
- Static Records without visibility between inspections

### Single Risk/Asset Tools
- Fracta, VODA.ai, Kondo
- One failure mode. Blind to network-wide change

### Hazard Models
- RMS (Moody's Analytics), Deltares
